How to Interpret the Results

Scoring Scale

  • +5 to +4: Very Strong Uptrend
  • +3 to +2: Moderate Uptrend
  • +1: Weak Uptrend
  • 0: Neutral/Consolidating
  • -1: Weak Downtrend
  • -2 to -3: Moderate Downtrend
  • -4 to -5: Very Strong Downtrend

Timeframe Weighting

The Combined score uses weighted averages: H4 (1x), D1 (2x), W1 (3x). This gives higher importance to longer timeframes, reflecting professional trading practice that weekly and daily trends are more significant than 4-hour trends.

Currency Strength

Each currency's strength is calculated by averaging all 7 pairs where it appears. For base currencies, uptrends = strength. For quote currencies, downtrends = strength (inverse logic).

Trading Strategy

Pair the strongest currency with the weakest one for optimal trade setup. For example, if USD is +4 and JPY is -3, USD/JPY is a strong buy signal.
